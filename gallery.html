"Back in March, me and my church buddies were eating dinner at Panera Bread like we usually do. The focus of discussion was the impact of social media on politics. Glen pointed out that the reason some people are so invested in politics nowadays is that they have nothing else going on in their lives, so they give their life meaning by attaching themselves to a greater cause. Months later, I realize what Glen said fully applies to me. I never had any permanent friends, I am an above average student with below average attributes like Autism, bad eyesight, and Sciatica. All of my romantic relationships are short-lived and I don't particularly care to devote my life to a profession since I am not obsessed with money. Instead, I have made a timeline of things I want to contribute to the future like prison ministry and reform, inventing eye prosthetics, and reuniting the Western and Eastern Orthodox Churches. Most people in life dedicate their lives to a family or a career. I decided to take a third option.

Sadly, I know exactly how the free blacks in 19th century America felt. Seeing so many members of your race suffer in bondage while you walk around unmolested. People thinking you are a criminal/slave because a large number of black men they have seen are criminals/slaves. Deathly afraid that one day you will end up like all the other blacks and have your freedom taken away. Slavery never ended, the only difference is that the plantation is now called prison.

I recieved my first kiss when I was seven years old. In order to annoy me, a blonde girl around my age and future homecoming queen smooched me on the lips. Me and the girl remained friends for years but we eventually went our seperate ways. In high school, I obtained "street cred" for having my first kiss with a hottie at such a young age, but when I really think about it, those few seconds of experience had about as much impact on my life as a gnat's fart. The kiss didn't really develop our relationship, nor did it make me a better or different person in anyway. I came to the realization that even though I could lose my virginity by sleeping with a hooker or something like that, I would be more or less the exact same person several months after the fact. Some lonely people think of virginity as a burden, but I think of it as information not any more relevant than a person's bloodtype.
